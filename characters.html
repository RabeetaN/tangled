Characters

Rapunzel is the princess of the Kingdom of Corona. As a child she was abducted by a wicked crone and locked away in a remote tower for eighteen years. She is found by chance by Flynn Rider

Flynn Rider made a name for himself as a notorious thief. However, after years of leading this lifestyle, he experiences a change of heart once he meets and falls in love with the lost princess, Rapunzel

Pascal is Rapunzel's pet chameleon, with the ability to change colours to camouflage and express himself. Pascal acts as Rapunzel's confidant and protector, with a fiesty and humorous personality

Maximus is a palace horse belonging to the Captain of the Corona Royal Guard. When the crown of the princess is stolen by Flynn Rider, Maximus embarks on a mission to retrieve it and punish the criminal

Mother Gothel is the wicked crone who retained her youth by the magical powers of a flower. When those powers are transferred to Rapunzel's hair, she kidnaps her and keeps her locked in a tower

The Stabbington brothers are a pair of thieves who once worked with Flynn Rider to steal the crown, before he betrayed them. The brothers form an alliance with mother Gothel to get their revenge
